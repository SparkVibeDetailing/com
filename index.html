Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

How long does detailing take?

Service duration depends on the package you choose. Our Deluxe Wash takes 1-2 hours, Ultimate Shine takes about 4 hours, and Platinum Works takes approximately 6 hours. Engine bay service adds about 2 hours to any package.

Do I need to be present during the service?

No, you don't need to be present. We come to your location and can work while you're at work or home. We just need access to water and electricity.

What products do you use?

We use only high-quality, professional-grade solutions that are safe for your vehicle. Our Sealer Hand Wax provides 6 months of protection, and all our cleaning agents are pH-balanced to protect your vehicle's surfaces.

How often should I get my car detailed?

We recommend our Deluxe Wash every 2-4 weeks, Ultimate Shine every 3-6 months, and Platinum Works annually. The Sealer Hand Wax protection lasts 6 months with proper maintenance.

Is the engine bay service safe for my vehicle?

Absolutely! Our trained technicians use specialized techniques and products to safely clean your engine bay without damaging sensitive components. We avoid direct spraying of electrical connections and use appropriate cleaners.
